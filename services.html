Services Offered

Everything a podcast needs to sound and look ready.

From the raw recording all the way to show notes and social clips. Each one below works fine on its own, or you can stack them into a full production package.

01

Audio Editing

Noise gone, levels balanced, dialogue cleaned up. The goal is every episode sounding the same from open to close, not just the good takes.

02

Podcast Editing

The full episode, produced start to finish. Cuts, pacing, intro and outro placement, structure. It comes back ready to publish.

03

Show Notes Writing

Show notes and episode descriptions written so new listeners can actually find the episode, not buried keyword soup.

04

Video Editing

Video podcast editing, intros, outros, ad cuts. Paced differently depending on whether it's YouTube, a talking-head format, or multiple speakers.

05

Podcast Content Repurposing

I go through the full episode and pull out the strongest moments as short-form clips, audiograms, and social-ready cuts.

06

Admin Assistance

Guest booking, scheduling, general production workflow. The stuff that keeps a weekly show actually weekly.

07

Social Media Management

Post scheduling and content coordination so the social side doesn't go quiet between episodes.

08

Graphic Designing

Cover art, YouTube thumbnails, layout design, all matched to whatever tone the show is actually going for.
