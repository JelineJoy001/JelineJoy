FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What days and hours do you usually work?

I typically work Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 8 PM (PH Time). I don't usually work weekends, but I do check messages and can handle urgent edits if arranged in advance.

How do we communicate during the project?

I'm flexible! Most of my clients prefer email, Slack, or Zoom for updates. I'll always keep you in the loop and provide progress updates so you know where we are in the editing process.

What do you need from me before starting?

Just your raw recordings, intro/outro files (if you have them), and any editing preferences. If you're new to podcasting, don't worry. I'll guide you step by step.

How many revisions are included?

Each package comes with 1–2 rounds of revisions per episode. I want to make sure you're happy with the final edit, so I'll always listen carefully to your feedback.
